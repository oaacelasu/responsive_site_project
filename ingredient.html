Punjabi Thali

There are main four food item in this thali.

  1. chapati
  2. Dal
  3. Rice
  4. Paneer Sabji

Paneer subji Ingredient

All ingredients of Paneer subji are as follow:
  • 400 gram paneer
  • 1/2 cup cashew paste
  • 1/2 cup onion
  • 1/2 cup tomato puree
  • 1/2 cup fresh creem
  • 2 table spoon red chili powder
  • 3-4 table spoon oil
  • 1/2 cup chopped capcicum
  • smooth pest of ginger, green chili
