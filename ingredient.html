Punjabi Thali

There are main four food item in this thali.

  1. Chapati
  2. Dal
  3. Rice
  4. Paneer Sabji

Paneer subji Ingredient

All ingredients of Paneer subji are as follow:
  • 400 gram paneer
  • 1/2 cup cashew paste
  • 1/2 cup onion
  • 1/2 cup tomato puree
  • 1/2 cup fresh creem
  • 2 table spoon red chili powder
  • 3-4 table spoon oil
  • 1/2 cup chopped capcicum
  • smooth pest of ginger, green chili

How to cook Paneer Bhurji

To make Paneer bhurji, first of all, you need to make a tomato puree; for that need to cook the tomato in boiled water for about five minutes.

Heat 1 tablespoon of oil in a pan on medium heat. Once it is hot, then add whole spices (bay leaf, dried chilli, cloves, peppercorns, cardamom).

Cook it for a few seconds. You will get a pleasant aroma of spices. After that, add roughly chopped ginger, garlic, and green chilli and cook it for one to two minutes.

Mix in roughly chopped tomatoes. Cook it until the tomatoes are soft. Let the mixture cool down slightly. While it is cooling, grate the paneer.

Now, make a smooth puree out of cooked tomatoes. I have used the same pan. Wipe it clean using a paper towel.

Heat remaining oil in a pan on medium heat. Once hot, add cumin seeds. Let them sizzle.

Then add chopped onions and a little salt to speed up the process. Cook until the onion gets light brown in colour. Add prepared tomato gravy and mix.

Cook until all the moisture evaporates. It may splutter a lot while cooking. So, you can partially cover the pan with a lid. It should start leaving the sides of the pan when cooked.

Add red chilli powder, coriander powder, and cumin powder.

Add water to make gravy consistency. Leave it for five minutes. Meantime capsicum will get cooked. Add grated paneer and stir.

Simmer for 2-3 minutes only and adjust the gravy consistency by adding water if needed. Add heavy cream. Stir well and turn off the stove. Garnish with chopped coriander and a slice of tomato.
